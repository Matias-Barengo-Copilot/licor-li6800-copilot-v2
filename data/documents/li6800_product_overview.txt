LI-6800 Portable Photosynthesis System — Product Overview
Source: licor.com/env/products/photosynthesis/LI-6800/

The LI-6800 is the global standard for photosynthetic gas exchange and chlorophyll a fluorescence measurements.

PRODUCT OVERVIEW

The LI-6800 simultaneously measures photosynthetic gas exchange and chlorophyll a fluorescence across identical plant and aquatic sample areas. The system extends to measure gas exchange from soil, insects, and various other sample types for diverse experimental applications.

ANSWER QUESTIONS WITH CONFIDENCE

Focus on your data: Fluorometer, file, calibration, balanced flow, and utilities assist data collection.

Design your experiments: Custom gas mixtures, auxiliary sensors, custom chambers, and extensive programming capabilities provide flexibility.

Take it comfortably into the field: Ergonomic grips, carrying harness, tripods, and monopods facilitate fieldwork.

PUBLISH YOUR RESEARCH STORY

The LI-6800 features supporting publishable data quality:
- Automated startup and system tests with real-time operating status
- Long-lasting, hot-swappable batteries with charge status indicators
- Responsive touch screen with powerful graphics processor
- Charts displaying live and logged data with auto-scaling capabilities
- Configurable data logging for comprehensive analysis
- Plain text and Microsoft Excel files with embedded equations
- Configuration management tools for storing and sharing protocols

NOVEL ADVANCEMENTS

Aquatic Photosynthesis: The 6800-18 Aquatic Chamber measures steady-state carbon assimilation and chlorophyll a fluorescence from algal suspensions, coral, macroalgae, seagrasses, and additional aquatic organisms.

Dynamic Assimilation Technique: Based on reformulated traditional assimilation equations, this allows measurements when chamber conditions are not at steady state, enabling faster CO2 response curves and survey measurements.

Multiphase Flash Fluorometer: Features uniform light output, configurable modulation rate, signal-to-noise optimization, and flash intensity up to 16,000 µmol, achieving maximum fluorescence yield with true OJIP point resolution.

COLLABORATION

The LI-6800 supports collaboration across undergraduate teaching colleges, research institutions, and non-profit organizations. Disciplines using the system include plant physiology, aquatic photosynthesis, climate science, soil science, and agronomy.

EXTEND RESEARCH

Users can measure soil flux to study soil-plant dynamics and scale up to field measurements using eddy covariance systems through LI-COR ecosystem monitoring solutions.

BUILT ON EXPERIENCE

The LI-6800 was developed by experienced photosynthesis researchers and engineers in collaboration with leading plant physiologists. It combines patented technologies with proven techniques. LI-COR photosynthesis instruments are the most cited in published literature, earning researcher trust for nearly four decades.

TRAINING AND SUPPORT

Resources include in-person and online training with scientists, videos, publications, comprehensive documentation, and technical support. Each LI-6800 includes two training certificates.

CONNECTIVITY

Users can connect the LI-6800 to computers, tablets, or phones via VNC viewer to plot sample responses in real time, modify settings, and view charts. Classroom projection capabilities enable sharing instrument screens with students.
